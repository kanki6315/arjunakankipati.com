Cicada Studios

Bucknell Senior Design Project

Cicada Studios is a team in Bucknell's single-semester Computer Science Senior Design Project (CSCI 479). Join us as we show you what we have been working on for the last three months.

Find Out More

What were we trying to do?

Our goal was to streamline communication for small college groups. Whereas many currently use a combination of various email threads and a number of different group chats, why not have them all in one place? Why not create an app with two different communication options, a forum and a chat? Thus, Canopy was born.

What did we produce?

Canopy is a communication platform directly targeted for college organizations. With our first implementation of our forum and chat Functionality, we have provided the essential communication tools for college students on campus. Currently, we have the ability on our client app to switch between multiple active organizations, participate in multiple group chats, and even theme the app depending on the active organization! Future improvements will include tags for subforums, as well as push notifications and a dashboard view for better contextual information!

Our iOS App

Login via Facebook

Login in easily by using your Facebook Account! More login options to come.

Forum

Post in the forum freely and also reply or vote as you want! Use this for your long term communication.

Chat

Create new group chats, and invite whoever you want! Chat on the go wherever you are!

Directory

Get information about others in your organization, and directly text or call them from within the app!

Things to come!

Upload/Send Multimedia

Send your friends your favorite images and videos! Also directly link your favorite GIFs and YouTube links!

Tags

Use tags to limit your conversations in the forum to specific people! Tags can only be created by users with sufficient access level, so set up your organization however you want!

Push Notifications

Get notified immediately when a new chat message appears or when someone replies to your post! Choose what to be notified about, and even customize the times you will receive notifications!

Dashboard Views

Open the app and see what conversations need your attention immediately! Choose from several different options to suit your personal choice!
